About Me

Building machines
that think and move

I'm a Mechatronics Engineering graduate from Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM) with a passion for building intelligent autonomous systems — from drones navigating obstacle fields to unmanned vessels patrolling the ocean.

Currently working as a Research Assistant at USM's marine robotics lab, I design and integrate hardware-software stacks for real-world robotic platforms. My work spans ArduPilot/PX4 autopilots, Jetson Nano perception pipelines, ROS 2 middleware, and custom PCB design.

I'm driven by the challenge of making autonomous systems robust, safe, and genuinely useful — whether that's a drone avoiding power lines or a surface vessel navigating a busy port.
